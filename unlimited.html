Regal Unlimited™ Program Details

With an annual Regal Unlimited™ movie subscription pass, you can watch as many movies as you want, as many times as you want, whenever you want, wherever you want. Plus, get advance tickets as soon as they go on sale, enjoy 10% off all food and non-alcoholic beverages in theatre. Plus, enjoy many Regal Crown Club benefits.

How To Join

  • Download the free Regal App.

  • Click on any Regal Unlimited™ banner and follow the steps.

  • Pay in full or month to month by debit or credit card.

Once sign up is complete, get your Unlimited ticket on the app, at the theatre box office, or theatre kiosks and get into the movies!

Program Perks

  • 10% off all food and non-alcoholic drink purchases

  • No blackout dates Watch as many movies as you want, anytime you want

  • Free large popcorn and soft drink on your birthday.

  • Earn Regal Crown Club® credits with every dollar spent using your Regal Unlimited™ subscription.

  • Be the first to see it with Regal Unlimited® screenings
